Ranked List of My Hobbies

Gaming

In the world of pixels and chaos, I find my battlefield. Like Guts wielding the Dragon Slayer, I wield my controller — not to escape, but to conquer. Every boss fight is a reflection of inner struggle, and every respawn is a reminder that I refuse to stay down.

Jogging

When the weight of the world gets too heavy, I run. Not from my demons, but with them. Each step, like Guts trudging through mud and blood, is a declaration — I will keep moving forward, no matter the pain.

Basketball

Basketball isn't just a game. It's a war zone. Strategy, pressure, grit — every match reminds me of Guts in the heat of battle. You push, you fight, you rise, or you fall. But either way, you leave it all on the court.

Eating

Even the Struggler must feed. Eating isn’t indulgence — it’s survival. A moment of calm in a world that never stops tearing. Like Guts resting between wars, I eat to regain strength… to fight again.

Sleeping

Sleep — the only time I don’t have to struggle. But even in dreams, the Brand burns. Like Guts haunted by demons in his rest, I find no true peace. Still, I close my eyes, because tomorrow, I fight again.
